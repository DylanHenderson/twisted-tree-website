## Link your Google Calendar to Simple Calendar

<Aside type="tip" title="Tip">
Grabbing your Google Calendar ID is the one step required for setting up Simple Calendar
</Aside>

### Adding the app to Shopify


After installation, go to your online store.

<Image src={cal1} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

Click customize for the theme you want to edit.

<Image src={cal2} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

Click to add a new section, either on the left or inbetween sections on the right

<Image src={cal3} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

Choose Simple Calendar from the Apps tab

<Image src={cal4} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

Your initial Calendar will be empty, you need to paste your public Google calendar ID where indicated. See below on how to retrieve or create that.

<Image src={cal5} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

### Creating or retrieving a Google Calendar ID

Go to your Google Calendars https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r
Click the + icon to create a new calendar if you need to. Follow the steps to create.


<Image src={cal6} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

Afterwards click the 3 dots that appear next to an existing calendar and click "Settings and sharing"

<Image src={cal7} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />


You'll see setting "Access permissions for events" as seen below. You need to make sure "Make available to public" is enabled so that your customers can see your events.
You also need to make sure "See all event details" is selected.

<Image src={cal8} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

<Aside type="caution" title="Note">
No events will show on your google calendar if you do not make it public
</Aside>

Copy the google calendar circled in red into the field in the Simple Calendar app.

<Image src={cal9} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />

<Aside type="caution" title="Note">
Use the calendar ID, not any of the other links.
</Aside>

Enter it as seen below.

<Image src={cal5} alt="Online Store" style={{  marginRight: '10px' }} />
